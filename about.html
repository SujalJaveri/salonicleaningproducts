About Saloni Cleaning Products

Women-led cleaning products company established in 2011, focused on quality, trust, and practical cleaning products in India.

Cleaning products that support better businesses.

Saloni Cleaning Products began with a clear purpose: offer practical, high-quality cleaning products that customers can trust. The company is women-led and continues to focus on safe, effective, and dependable formulations for homes, shops, offices, and commercial users.

Our product range is designed to be simple to understand and easy to sell. From floor cleaners and toilet cleaners to liquid soap, detergent, dishwash gel, room freshener, and fabric conditioner, every product is made with everyday use in mind.
